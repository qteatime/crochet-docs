=====================
A Crocheted Adventure
=====================

You've loved story-books for as far as you can remember. Especially
the ones that let you *change* how the story was told. What were
they called again? Inventive books? No, not quite. Interactive books?
Yes. That one.

Going from your memories, the way they worked was that they'd
contain some story portion, and then they'd present choices that
let you move progress through it. They said modern ones---in computers---even
let you :doc:`choose things by clicking these... links? <intro/001>`
